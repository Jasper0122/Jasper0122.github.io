Bio

Current, I am seeking a Ph.D. position in GeoAI & Urban Science, starting Fall 2025. If you are interested in my research or would like to collaborate, please feel free to reach out.

Hi! I’m Zongrong (Jasper) Li, a master’s student at the University of Southern California, majoring in Spatial Data Science. My passion lies in advancing our understanding of Urban Science through innovative approaches, using geographic knowledge to inform and enhance developments in AI. I’m particularly fascinated by the intricate integration of geospatial data, including remote sensing and street view imagery, with large language models.

My goal is to contribute novel tools, algorithms, and general methodologies that will empower future researchers to explore Urban Science more effectively.
